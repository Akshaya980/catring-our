Preserving Tamil Culinary Traditions Since 2005

SPR brings generations of culinary expertise to your special occasions. Our family has been serving authentic Tamil cuisine in Chennai and across Tamil Nadu for over 25 years.

  • Traditional recipes passed down through generations
  • 100% hygienic preparation and service
  • Customizable menus for all budgets
  • Both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options
